Our Products

Various kinds of adhesive papers available
Contact us for more detailed informations



PP Paper

PP paper is a durable, water-resistant synthetic paper made from polypropylene. It's tear-resistant, recyclable, and commonly used for labels, packaging, and outdoor signage.

Semi Gloss Paper

Semi-gloss paper is a coated paper with a subtle shine, offering good color with reduced glare.It's ideal for printing photos and marketing materials with a professional finish.

PET Silver Paper

PET silver paper is a metallic,polyester-based paper with a silver finish.It’s very durable and often used for various applications requiring a sleek,reflective surface.




PET Gold Paper

PET Gold paper is a polyester-based paper with a gold metallic finish, used for luxury packaging, labels, and decorative applications requiring a durable, reflective surface.

Rainbow Paper

Rainbow paper is a colorful, multi-hued paper often used in crafts, stationery, and art projects, featuring a gradient or striped pattern of various colors.

Hologram paper

Hologram paper is a reflective, iridescent paper that displays 3D-like patterns and colors, used in packaging, crafts, and security labels for visual effects.




Transparance Paper

Transparency paper is a clear, plastic-like sheet used for overhead projections, overlays, and crafts, allowing light to pass through for displaying images or text.

Security Paper

Security paper is specialized paper embedded with features like watermarks, micro-printing, or security threads to prevent forgery or unauthorized copying of important documents.

Lamination Gloss Paper

Lamination gloss paper is coated with a glossy laminate layer, enhancing durability, water resistance, and color vibrancy, often used for high-quality prints and protective covers.




Lamination Matte Paper

Lamination matt paper features a matte laminate finish, providing a non-reflective, smooth surface that resists fingerprints and enhances durability for high-quality prints and documents.

Hot Stamping paper gold/silver

Hot stamping paper is specially coated to accept foil transfer during hot stamping, creating high-quality, embossed designs with metallic or colored finishes for added visual impact.
